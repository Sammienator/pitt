Company History

Inlight Entertainment was founded by Peter "PITT" Rohi, a native of Kitengela,Nairobi KENYA where music was born and continues to live. Inlight Entertainement is owned and operated by Peter Rohi & Paul Gateri. Paul and Peter have both worked in the Entertainement Industry for a combined 12 years and both have had the opportunity to perfom mentor and motivate artists, muscicians, poets and designers for well over a decade. They have performed in numerous musical events known to the industry.

Founding Fathers

Because of Peter and Paul's hands on expericence, they take great pride and extra special care in what they do. They make immeasurable strides to committ to customer satisfaction and both aim for one thing only.....and that is to please every single client.
